Process

UI design is something that I am immensely passionate about. I love nothing more than concepting, sketching, wireframing & prototyping beautiful interfaces.

I obsess over simple interfaces & find myself analysing ticket machines & ATMs, redesigning them in my mind & using them as inspiration for my day job. I constantly think about user flow, relevant information & simplification of a process.

When asked to translate a complex process into a simple user interface, I start by designing a flow diagram and make sure to constantly consider relevancy & conditional information. From there I begin sketching in my dot grid pad. This allows me to gain a tactile understanding of the elements that I plan to use to complete the task. Once complete, I will begin to wireframe & a crude prototype that I will then present.
